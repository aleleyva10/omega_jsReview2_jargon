Jargon

  • . - dot
  • () - parenthesis, parens
  • [] - brackets, square brackets, square braces
  • {} - braces, curlies, curly braces, curly brackets
  • < - less than, bird
  • > - greather than, gator
  • / - slash, whack, forward slash
  • \ - back slash
  • ! - not, bang
  • # - pound, hash
  • * - star, splat
  • || - or, pipes
  • $ - dollar sign, bling, cash
  • NaN - not a number, bread
